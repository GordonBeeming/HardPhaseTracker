### Description

**Track your fasting journey with simplicity and precision.**

HardPhase Tracker is a minimalist fasting app designed for intermittent fasting enthusiasts. Whether you're doing 16/8, OMAD, 5:2, or alternate-day fasting, this app helps you stay on track.

**Features:**
• Multiple fasting schedules (16/8, 18/6, 20/4, OMAD, 5:2, ADF, and more)
• Custom eating windows tailored to your lifestyle
• Meal logging with reusable templates
• Weight trend visualization (integrates with Apple Health)
• Electrolyte tracking checklist
• Clean, distraction-free interface
• Full iPad support
• Dark mode compatible

**Apple Health Integration:**
HardPhase Tracker reads your weight, body fat percentage, and sleep data from Apple Health to show personalized trends. Your health data stays private and secure on your device.

**Privacy First:**
All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, share, or sell your information.

Perfect for anyone practicing intermittent fasting, time-restricted eating, or working towards metabolic health goals.
